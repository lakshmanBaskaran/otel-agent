# Reading the Forward Book

## What OTB means
Future stay dates, cancellations excluded.

## How to read it
Revenue, room nights, and ADR only make sense together. Any one alone is not enough.

## Thin vs healthy
Don't use absolute thresholds — compare months against each other.
- A month with less than half the average across future months → thin
- A month above the average → healthy
- A month significantly above the average → peak

The current month is partially historical. The furthest-out months naturally show lower numbers because they haven't picked up yet.

## ADR signals
Compare each month's ADR against the cross-month average. Don't make absolute judgments — read it relative to the rest of the book.

## Don't compare in isolation
Two months at €35K and €28K only means something if you know the seasonality. Peak vs shoulder changes everything.

## Recommendations
- Thin month within 30 days → urgent
- Thin month within 60 days → time to build
- Strong peak month → protect rate
- ADR slipping → check pickup mix, likely OTA-heavy
