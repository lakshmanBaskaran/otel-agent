# Group Block Management

## Why this matters
Group bookings can provide big revenue, but they also expose the hotel to high concentration risk.

## How to read it
- Group share of total revenue
- Group share by month — which months are group-heavy
- Single group dependency → is one group carrying the whole month?
- Group ADR vs Transient ADR — groups are usually lower

## Red flags
- A single group share covering over half a month → high exposure
- One block over a fifth of a month → single point of failure
- Group ADR much lower than transient → displacing better-paying business

## The trap
Don't celebrate high group revenue without checking concentration. A €20K block looks great until it cancels.

But it's not all negative — group bookings drive volume and fill mid-week dates that transient won't.

## Recommendations
- Group-heavy month → confirm deposits, add attrition clauses
- One block dominant → have a backfill plan ready
- Group ADR very low → check if it's displacing better business
