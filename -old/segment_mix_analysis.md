# Segment Concentration

## What segment mix is
The breakdown of revenue by market code. It shows which source is giving what amount of revenue to the hotel.

## Macro groups matter more than individual codes
Groups like Retail, Corporate, MICE, and Leisure/Event should be given importance first. Individual codes matter only after understanding the high-level picture.

## What a healthy mix looks like
An equal share across the macro groups. No single segment should be dominating.

## Red flags
- Any single segment taking a big share
- Corporate barely present → missing stable base business
- BAR is tiny → direct retail pricing isn't working
- OTA is the largest segment → rate erosion and commission bleed will happen

## How to answer "what's driving July?"
This is a classic question. Answer in layers:
1. Total revenue and room nights for the month
2. Top 3 segments by revenue share
3. Group vs Transient split
4. Flag any outsized concentration
5. Be transparent — check all the data before reaching a conclusion
6. Check pickup — is the dominant segment still growing?

## Recommendations
- Over-concentrated in group → "If X cancels we lose €Y. Backfill plan needed."
- Weak corporate → "Sales team should target more corporate accounts."
- Strong BAR ADR vs OTA → "Shift mix toward direct. Each booking saved is commission saved."
