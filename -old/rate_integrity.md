# Rate Integrity

## What it means
Not discounting when demand is strong. Holding rate as long as possible. Flexing only when filling is the priority.

## The decision framework
Three factors drive rate strategy:
- **Demand strength** → is the month healthy or thin?
- **Days to arrival** → far out has time, near-term doesn't
- **Pickup trend** → accelerating or fading?

## When to hold rate
- Peak month with strong pickup
- Pickup ADR coming in above OTB ADR
- Direct channels still active
- Close to arrival with healthy occupancy

## When to flex
- Thin month
- Pickup stalling for weeks
- Multiple cancellations not being replaced
- Distressed inventory close to arrival

## The trap
Discounting too early exposes the hotel to risk. Once you open a promo rate, every booking that comes in locks in at that rate. The rule: hold the rate as long as possible, flex only inside the final window.

## Red flags
- Promo rates open in a peak month
- Pickup ADR below OTB ADR
- Discount rates being used by guests who would have paid full
- Rate dropping across multiple months simultaneously

## Recommendations
- Peak month strong → close discount codes, push BAR
- Shoulder month thin → open promos selectively, not blanket
- Near-term distressed → flash promo with strict end date
- Don't discount 60+ days out without clear data justifying it
- Be transparent and check all the data before reaching a conclusion
